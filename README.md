Survival Arena
===============

Main Scene
---------
`3DGB-SurvivalArena\Assets\StarterAssets\ThirdPersonController\Scenes\Playground.unity`

Controls
--------
- `WASD` to move
- `SPACE` to jump
- `E` to open doors
- `G` to throw grenades

Item Locations
--------------
- Hovering bombs and hearts are scattered around the map; they can increase or decrease HP when collected.
- The key sits on top of the building and opens the green glowing door.
- Grab the gold stash to trigger the victory screen.

Known Issues
------------
- Colliders on NavMeshAgents can cause odd interactions with thrown grenades (strange bounces/hits).
